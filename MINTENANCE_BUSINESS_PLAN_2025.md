## 📋 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity

The UK home maintenance market is valued at **£40 billion annually**, yet homeowners struggle to find trustworthy contractors, accurately assess repair costs, and manage home maintenance jobs. Meanwhile, skilled contractors waste time on unqualified leads and face barriers to growing their businesses.

Mintenance solves this with an **AI-powered contractor discovery marketplace** that:
- Instantly assesses property damage using GPT-4 Vision and custom ML models
- Matches homeowners with verified contractors using intelligent algorithms
- Protects payments through escrow and provides real-time job management
- Automates workflows with 13 specialized AI agents

### The Product

**Platform:** Web + iOS + Android applications
**Technology:** Next.js 16, React Native 0.76, Supabase, AI/ML (OpenAI, YOLO, SAM 3)
**Status:** Production-ready with 87.7% test coverage
**Unique Advantage:** Only marketplace combining GPT-4 Vision, local ML models, and autonomous AI agents

### Market Traction

- ✅ Platform ready to scale

**Target (Month 18):**

## 🎯 PROBLEM & SOLUTION

### The Problem

#### For Homeowners:
1. **Uncertainty About Damage** - Can't assess severity or cost (±50% error)
2. **Finding Contractors** - Difficult to find qualified, trustworthy professionals
3. **Price Transparency** - No idea if quotes are fair or inflated
4. **Payment Risk** - Fear of paying upfront and contractor disappearing
5. **Communication Gaps** - Missed calls, delayed responses, no central communication

#### For Contractors:
1. **Unqualified Leads** - 70% of inquiries waste their time
2. **Pricing Difficulty** - Hard to price jobs competitively without historical data
3. **Payment Delays** - Chasing payments wastes 10-15 hours/month
4. **Marketing Costs** - High CAC on Google Ads, Facebook (£50-100 per lead)
5. **Admin Burden** - Invoicing, scheduling, documentation takes 25% of time

### The Mintenance Solution

#### For Homeowners:
✅ **AI Damage Assessment** - Upload photos, get instant analysis (85% accurate, £0.25/job)
✅ **Verified Contractors** - Background checks, certifications, reviews, portfolios
✅ **Transparent Pricing** - AI cost estimates + multiple bids for comparison
✅ **Escrow Protection** - Funds held securely until job completion
✅ **Real-Time Communication** - In-app messaging, notifications, job tracking

#### For Contractors:
✅ **Pre-Qualified Jobs** - AI matches based on skills, location, availability (80% match rate)
✅ **AI Pricing Tool** - Smart bid suggestions based on market data (+15% win rate)
✅ **Guaranteed Payments** - Escrow ensures payment upon completion
✅ **Low-Cost Leads** - Platform fee model (£25/job) vs £50-100 ads
✅ **Automated Admin** - Invoicing, scheduling, documentation handled by platform
