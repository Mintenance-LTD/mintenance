## SECTION 2, The problem (so they feel understood)

### Hiring someone to work on your home shouldn't feel like a risk.

But it does. You ring round for callbacks that never come. You get three different prices for the
same job. You hand over a deposit and hope. And good tradespeople have it just as bad, chasing leads
that ghost them and invoices that never get paid.

**We're fixing both sides of that.**

---

## SECTION 3, How it works (3 steps)

**1. Post your job** Tell us what needs doing. We match you with vetted local trades who actually
want the work.

**2. Agree the price up front** Quotes come in writing, before anything starts. No surprises, no
"while I'm here" add-ons.

**3. Pay safely, release when it's done** Your money is held securely and only released when _you_
confirm the job's done right. No deposit black holes.

## SECTION 4, Why founding members

### Why we're giving 150 seats away free, for life

We'd rather build Mintenance _with_ the first people who use it than guess what they need. Founding
members get full access free, forever, in return for honest feedback as we grow.

**You get:**

- ✅ Lifetime-free access, no fees, ever
- ✅ First pick of vetted [city] trades as we onboard them
- ✅ A direct line to the founders, your feedback shapes the product
- ✅ Founding-member status when we launch publicly

**Homeowners & landlords:** stop gambling on the next tradesperson you Google. **Tradespeople:**
stop chasing leads and unpaid invoices.

## Implementation notes

- **Capture postcode**, it's your single most valuable field. It tells you where density is forming
  and which city/area to double down on.
- **Connect the form to a sheet** (Google Sheets / Airtable) so the weekly numbers reminder has live
  data to pull from.
- **Update `[N]` regularly**, a visibly shrinking counter does the persuasion for you for free.
- **One link everywhere:** this page is the destination for every "link in bio" and every CTA in the
  20-post bank.
- **Don't gate it behind a fancy build.** A clean Carrd page or a Typeform with this copy will
  convert your first 150 fine. Ship it this week.
